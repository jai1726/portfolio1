1.MNNITIAN

A student interaction web application created by group of 4 people that allows students in our college to connect and share interview experiences.

It is a responsive full-stack project with features that include finding students with particular skills, sharing experiences, reading experiences and request to connect which decreases the time by 75% to find people.

Tech Stack :JavaScript, React, MongoDB, ExpressJs,NodeJs, Html and CSS.

E-Diary Web Application

Developed a good-looking web application to keep track of daily tasks.

Project was used by my group of friends to manage their tasks and track their daily activities.

Every entry is limited to 100 characters to maintain uniformity, you can access the full entry by clicking read more.

Tech Stack :JavaScript,ExpressJs,NodeJs, Html and CSS.
